# 🚀 ZeleraDeck

**Empowering small businesses to go digital in seconds.**

ZeleraDeck is a powerful SaaS platform designed specifically for local shops, boutiques, and small business owners. We bridge the gap between offline sales and online presence by providing a lightning-fast, mobile-first storefront solution that integrates directly with WhatsApp.

---

## ✨ Why ZeleraDeck?

Traditional e-commerce is complex, expensive, and overwhelming for small vendors. ZeleraDeck simplifies everything:

- **⚡ Instant Storefront:** Launch your professional online catalogue in less than 60 seconds.
- **📱 Mobile-First Design:** Optimized for the modern customer who shops on the go.
- **💬 WhatsApp Integration:** Orders land directly in your WhatsApp inbox, preserving the personal touch of local commerce.
- **🖼️ Easy Management:** Add products, update prices, and manage stock from a sleek, minimalist dashboard.
- **🔍 Smart Search & Discovery:** Helps your customers find exactly what they need with our high-performance search engine.
- **🏷️ QR Code Ready:** Every shop gets a unique QR code. Print it, stick it on your counter, and let walk-in customers browse your full digital catalogue.

---

## 🛠️ Performance & Tech

ZeleraDeck is built with cutting-edge technology to ensure speed, security, and scalability:

- **Frontend:** React + Vite + Tailwind CSS v4 (Premium UI/UX)
- **State Management:** TanStack Query for seamless data synchronization
- **Backend:** Django Research Framework (REST API)
- **Deployment:** Vercel (Frontend) & Render (Backend)
