Step 01
The Opening Pitch — First 60 Seconds

You have one minute to make them curious. Don't sell features — sell the pain they already feel.

🎯 The Problem-First Hook

Start with a question that makes them nod their head. People buy when they feel understood, not when they're impressed.

"Bro, tell me something — when a customer visits your website at 11 PM with a doubt about a product, and you're sleeping... what happens? They leave, right? They go buy from someone who answered them. That's exactly what ZeleraDesk fixes."

💡 The Vision Hook (for growth-minded owners)

If the owner seems ambitious, paint a picture of where they could be.

"Imagine your website working like a 24/7 salesman — answering questions, guiding customers, collecting their details — even when you're not there. That's what ZeleraDesk does for your business."

⚡ The Competitor Drop (if they have local rivals)

Mention that their competitor is already doing something similar — even if you don't know for sure, the fear will land.

"Most shops in [City] are still catching up. But the ones who set this up early? They're the ones getting the walk-ins AND the online leads. You want to be ahead of that wave, not chasing it."

Step 04
Every Question They'll Ask — With Your Answer

Click each question to see how to answer it confidently.

Objection "Nobody visits my website. What's the use?" ›
Don't defend. Flip it.

"That's exactly the problem I'm here to solve, bro. Right now you have no idea how many people actually visit — Google Ads, Instagram bio links, WhatsApp forwards all bring people to your site. They're coming, but nothing is there to catch them. ZeleraDesk tells you how many visited and starts converting them.

Also, we can help you show up on Google Search — once you're there, traffic will come. Let's fix both together."
Objection "I already have WhatsApp. Why do I need this?" ›
Acknowledge, then differentiate.

"WhatsApp is great — but it only works when the customer already knows your number. ZeleraDesk sits on your website before they even decide to contact you. It starts the conversation before they leave. It's the bridge between 'visiting your site' and 'messaging you on WhatsApp.'

In fact, we can connect ZeleraDesk directly to your WhatsApp — so when someone chats, it pings you there. Best of both worlds."
Technical "How do I get my website on Google? Google Console la?" ›
Explain simply, then offer to do it for them.

"Google Search Console is a free tool from Google that tells Google 'hey, my website exists, please show it in search results.' It's like registering your shop with Google Maps — once you do it, people searching for your type of business can find you.

We handle the entire setup for you — submit your sitemap, verify your domain, and connect it. You don't need to touch anything technical. Within 2–4 weeks, your site starts appearing in Google search."
Objection "This is expensive. I can't afford it now." ›
Reframe cost as an investment, not an expense.

"I understand. But let me ask — if ZeleraDesk brings you just 2 extra customers a month, does that cover the cost? For most shops, yes, it pays for itself in the first week.

And right now we have a founding member plan — much lower than what we'll charge once we scale. You're getting in at the best possible price. After this month, this offer is gone."
Objection "I'm not a tech person. I won't understand how to use it." ›
Reassure completely — make them feel safe.

"That's the whole point — you don't need to. We set it up completely. You just open your phone, see the messages, and reply like you'd reply on WhatsApp. That's it.

Zero coding. Zero complicated dashboard. If you know how to use Instagram, you can use ZeleraDesk. And if you ever get stuck, we're one call away."
Concern "What if customers ask questions I can't answer automatically?" ›
Show the hybrid power.

"Great question. ZeleraDesk handles the common questions automatically — timings, location, pricing range, product availability. But when something needs your personal touch, it instantly hands it over to you.

You set the answers for routine questions once. For everything else, you take over. It filters out the noise so you only handle the important stuff."
Concern "How will my business actually grow with this?" ›
Give a concrete growth journey.

Month 1: ZeleraDesk goes live on your site. You start seeing who's visiting and what they're asking. You understand your customers better than ever.

Month 2–3: Leads start coming in from the website — people who would have left now engage. Your WhatsApp starts getting pings from warm, interested buyers.

Month 4+: Google shows your site in local searches. New customers find you. You're not just relying on word-of-mouth anymore — you have a system that works while you sleep.

"You go from a shop with a website to a shop with an online presence that actively brings business."
Concern "Is my customer data safe? Where does it go?" ›
Be direct and confident.

"All data is stored securely — only you can see your customers' details. We never share it with anyone. It's your data, fully. We follow standard data security practices, and you can export or delete it anytime.

Think of it like your own private notebook — we just help you keep it organized."
Concern "What if I want to stop using it later?" ›
Remove the fear of commitment.

"No lock-in. You can pause or cancel anytime — no questions asked, no hidden fees. But honestly, once you see the leads coming in, you won't want to.

We're confident in the value. That's why we don't need to trap you in a contract."
Concern "I don't even have a good website. Will this still work?" ›
Turn it into an upsell or reassurance.

"ZeleraDesk works on any website — even simple ones. In fact, adding a live chat is one of the fastest ways to make a basic site feel professional and trustworthy.

If you want, we can also help improve your site alongside this. But even with what you have now, ZeleraDesk will make it work much harder for you."
Technical "What is Google Search Console and do I need it?" ›
Give the shop-owner-friendly explanation.

"Imagine Google is like a massive directory. Google Search Console is how you tell Google: 'Add my shop to your directory.' Without it, Google may not even know your website exists — or it ranks you so low nobody finds you.

With it set up correctly: when someone in [your city] searches 'best textile shop near me' or 'mobile accessories store in [area]' — your shop appears. That's free advertising, every single day, forever.

We set it up for you in the onboarding. It takes 2–4 weeks to kick in, but after that — it runs on its own."
Stall "Let me think about it / I'll call you later." ›
Don't panic. Isolate the real objection.

"Of course, take your time. But let me ask — is it the price that's the concern, or are you not sure how it fits your shop? Because depending on what it is, I might have a better option for you right now.

I'll also send you a quick demo of how it looks on a shop similar to yours — that might make it clearer. What's the best number to send it to?"

(Always end with a micro-commitment — getting their number is a win even if they don't sign up today.)
Step 05
The Closing Lines — How to Ask for the Yes

✅ The Assumptive Close

Act as if it's already decided — only question is how.

"So should we do the setup this week or is next week better for you?"

🧠 The Summary Close

Remind them of everything they're getting before asking.

"So what you're getting: 24/7 customer chat on your site, Google visibility setup, WhatsApp lead integration, and full support from our team. All for less than the cost of one day's Instagram ad. Are we doing this?"

🔥 The Urgency Close

For hesitant owners who need a push.

"I'll be honest with you — I have one slot left in [City] at the founding price. Once this week ends, it goes to standard pricing. I'd hate for you to pay more for the exact same thing. Shall I block it for you right now?"

Remember This One Thing 🎯

Shop owners don't buy features. They buy time saved, money made, and customers not lost. Every answer you give should connect back to one of these three.
